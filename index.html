About Us

IC Aviation specialises in providing professional consultancy services in all areas of the airport business - from passenger growth to non-aeronautical development to operational enhancements. IC Aviation offers a complete package of services that will transform the future of your airport. Contact IC Aviation today and let us create the right strategy for the successful development of your airport.

In today's market, only airports that operate efficiently and competitively will continue to develop into profitable - yet sustainable - commercial ventures. IC Aviation boasts unrivalled experience and a proven track record in the successful development of airport businesses.

At IC Aviation, we believe that airports should be the focus of and drivers for economic growth - from the tourists they deliver to the employment they generate - airports should be at the centre of economic growth in their local economies. Contact us today to learn more about IC Aviation's unique consultancy services that will maximise an airport's value to local economies.
