The Future of Aviation in Northern Europe

IC Aviation runs regular information days and educational seminars. The next IC Aviation event "The Future of Aviation in Northern Europe" will take place on 11th and 12th March 2014 at Stockholm Arlanda.

The Future of Aviation in Northern Europe will be the first aviation conference of its kind to bring all of the major players in this market together in one place, at the same time. There will be keynote speakers from some of the most influential players in this market as well as an exciting panel debate featuring the CEO of Norwegian Airlines as well as senior executives from SAS, Finnair and Swedavia.

The event will also be hearing from the Russian market with the Chief Commercial Officer of St. Petersburg (Pulkovo) Airport, Mr. Evgeniy Ilyin, coming to speak about the future potential of the Russian market and the future growth plans of Pulkovo Airport.

The new Guidelines for State Aid to Airports, to be published by the European Commission later this year, are also a hot topic for many of Europe’s airports. The Future of Aviation in Northern Europe will have the leading aviation and state-aid lawyer Dennis Oswell from the Brussels law firm Oswell and Vahida giving us his expert opinion on the new legislation.

Anyone who is involved in the travel, aviation or tourism industries in Northern Europe cannot afford to miss this event.
