Passenger Growth

IC Aviation's expertise is unique in the industry. With a combined experience of 20 years working for a leading European airline and several European airports - IC Aviation knows what it takes to enhance every aspect of an airport's business. Commercial Development

The key to ensuring long term sustainability as an airport in today's market is to maximise every revenue stream - airports can no longer rely on aeronautical income as their main source of income - sustainable growth requires a strategic review of the entire business. Having developed and implemented successful development plans for several European airports, IC Aviation has a track record of delivering results that improve the commercial performance of the airport's business.
