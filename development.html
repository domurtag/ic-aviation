Airport Development

Passenger Growth

IC Aviation's expertise is unique in the industry. With 20 years experience working for one of the world's largest and most successful airlines as well as fo many regional airports - the team at IC Aviation understands what airlines are looking for. IC Aviation uses this unique knowledge to develop viable passenger growth plans for airports all over Europe. The team at IC Aviation also has extensive experience in negotiating major agreements on behalf of airports and airlines - IC Aviation knows what it takes to create a strategy for long term passenger growth for your airport.

Commercial Development

The key to ensuring long term sustainability as an airport in today's market is to maximise every revenue stream - airports can no longer rely on aeronautical income as their main source of income - sustainable growth requires a strategic review of the entire business. Having developed and implemented successful development plans for several European airports, IC Aviation has a track record of delivering results that improve the commercial performance of the airport's business.
